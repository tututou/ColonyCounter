ColonyCounter is a web app that is used to count the number of
yeast colonies in an image of a petri dish.

To upload an image to analyze:

1. Click the Side Panel (the 3 bars) in the top left.

2. Click "Process Images".

3. Click "Choose file" and select the image you wish to upload.
From here, you can change the file you have chosen or click "Submit".
Once you submit, the result page will appear.

4. To upload another image click the "Back" button.
